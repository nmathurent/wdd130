Cruises have become a popular vacation choice for many. But, with so many types of cruises to choose from, deciding which ship to embark on can seem like a daunting decision. Do you want your getaway on the high seas to explore foreign and exotic places? Or, are you looking for a relaxing experience to get some sun and see around you? With so many options on offer, choosing the right type of cruise vacation can take a lot of time, thought and planning.

River Cruise

If you're looking to visit more intimate corners of the world you can do that while travelling the world’s most famous rivers on small size ships that offer all the amenities of a modern hotel—restaurants, bars, fitness facilities, spas, internet access, and comfortable staterooms. These cruises offer to the passengers an itinerary full of unique destinations to discover.

Luxury

Discover the wide selection of cruises from the most select and exclusive companies. What characterizes luxury cruises are their small ships that arrive at ports where others cannot reach, a higher crew/passenger ratio, a timeless atmosphere, refined elegance and gastronomy of the highest level.

Premium Cruise

These types of cruise companies offer more space and service per passenger on medium ships that do not reach 2,000 passengers. Premium ships pride themselves on sophisticated options, offering personalized service, world-class cuisine and high-quality entertainment options.

Ocean cruises

Maritime cruises are usually large ocean liners between 3,000 and 4,000 passengers, there are some ships that can accommodate up to 6,000 passengers, who cross seas and oceans during their voyages. The facilities tend to be luxurious, with all kinds of first class services. The big sea cruises have an incredible entertainment offer on board, with activities ranging from big shows to rock climbing walls.

Solo Cruises

Singles cruises are fantastic opportunities to meet people, make friends, and hopefully find love. These trips are comfortable, practical and have everything a single person needs: men and women who want to meet more people.

Family cruises

They mostly offer family-specific programs if you are looking to enjoy an unforgettable family vacation. You won't have to worry about anything other than deciding what to do each day. From the moment you get up you will begin to enjoy endless food options included and activities and facilities designed for the enjoyment of your family, swimming pools, water parks, musicals or a surf pool.
