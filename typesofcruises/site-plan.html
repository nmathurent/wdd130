Overview

Purpose

With so many different types of cruises in the market, and some of them unknown to the public I have create this website to provide more information about the available options. The cruise lines fall into various categories, and the purpose of this website is to provide a guide that can help you to choose the right type of cruise for you.

Audience

Singles, families, couples and adventurers looking for unforgettable vacations while navigating the waters . My audience access my site thru desktop, laptop and mobile devices.

Content

Home page

Cruises have become a popular vacation choice for many. But, with so many types of cruises to choose from, deciding which ship to embark on can seem like a daunting decision. Do you want your getaway on the high seas to explore foreign and exotic places? Or, are you looking for a relaxing experience to get some sun and see around you? With so many options on offer, choosing the right type of cruise vacation can take a lot of time, thought and planning. Instead of looking for information in a bunch of different places, we've put together a handy guide below that walks you through the different cruise categories to help you select your perfect cruise.
